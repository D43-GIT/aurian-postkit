The research, short version

  • The name field is the main thing Instagram's own search reads, so it should carry what you do, not just who you are.
  • The bio is what people read, and what Google reads. Google has been indexing public professional accounts since July 2025, so the words in there do work outside Instagram.
  • Nobody has real proof that a 'work with me' line lifts clicks. Every number floating around traces back to a marketing blog with no study behind it. Treat it as a test we judge on our own numbers.
  • Instagram lets you add several links but only shows the first one, and native links give you no click data. One link we can measure beats five nobody sees.
  • The word 'premium' is deliberately avoided. A self-applied adjective reads as claiming. A number, a client name or a phone number reads as proving.
